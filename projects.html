Garden Rescue

Garden Rescue is an app that uses machine learning to tell the user if the plants in their garden are healthy, diseased, bug-eaten, or just dehydrated. It also gives information to the user on how to take care of their plant after it has been diagnosed, such as giving it more water if dehydrated.
